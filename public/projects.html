Firewall Penitentiary

In the near future, due to overpopulation and resource scarcity, the criminal justice system will receive an overhaul. Career criminals will be held in "virtual reality" prisons and made to fight to the death in simulated games.



Flight of the Rubicon (Work in Progress)

The year is 2161CE. Mankind has colonized most of the solar system and the homeworld exerts firm control over the colonies on Venus, Mars and the moons of Jupiter. Scientists on the red planet, however, have other ideas and Project Rubicon may very well change the course of the coming war.
